Amazon "R" Acronyms

  • RA: Retail Arbitrage – buying low to resell on Amazon
  • ROI: Return on Investment – profit relative to cost
  • ROAS: Return on Ad Spend – revenue generated per ad dollar
  • Referral Fee: Fee charged by Amazon on each sale
  • Return Rate / Returns: Customer returns metric
  • RSR: Reserve Stow Racks – secondary warehouse storage
